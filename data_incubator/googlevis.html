The Click Through Rate Vs Banner Position

In this bubble chart, the size of the bubble represent the number of records for each position of banner and the horizontal value is the click-throuh-rate. The position of the banner is coded as number from 1 to 7. We can see that the click-through-rate for ads in different position are very different.
